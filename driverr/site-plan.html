Overview

Purpose

Provide a way for users to track their car odometer and the fuel efficiency of their vehicle.

Audience

Car owners of any age. Also businesses that are trying to maintain their vehicles.

Dynamic elements

The javascript on the page will need to calculate average miles per gallon over different periods of time as well as create a view of all the vehicles the user has entered on the site.
